FAQs

What is Quiz-Wiz ?

Quiz-Wiz is a web application that uses AI technology to develop quiz questions for you based on a given subject.

How does Quiz-Wiz work ?

Simply enter a subject into the generate question box to begin studying, and it will generate questionnaires for that specific subject to aid you in your test preparation.

Why is Quiz-Wiz free ?

We made the decision to make it free for the benefit of the community because we are aware of how challenging it can be to be a student with significant debt that could accumulate during your education.

Can it generate questions for any topic ?

Quiz-Wiz will generate questions for any subject to optimize your studying so that you can ACE your next test!

Is the question generator reliable ?

The question generator will indeed generate the most favorable questions of your subject of choice, in order to enhance your studying.

How do I contact support ?

You can reach out to us by using our emails posted in the about page.
